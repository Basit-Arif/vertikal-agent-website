Our Story

Vertikal Agent was born from the challenges our founders faced while operating growth-stage companies. Manual follow-ups, spreadsheet-driven workflows, and fragmented tools slowed down teams everywhere. We set out to design agents that combine the reliability of enterprise systems with the flexibility of human-led processes.

Phase 1

Automation studio

We worked alongside operations teams to build bespoke automation playbooks—understanding the human context behind every task.

Phase 2

Agent platform

We standardized the best playbooks and wrapped them with learnable LLM-powered agents that observe, reason, and act across systems.

Today

Autonomous operations

Vertikal Agents now run critical workflows—sales follow-up, onboarding, financial reconciliation, support triage—for customers across 15+ industries.

What we stand for

  • Automation you can trust: Agents log every action and integrate with the systems you already rely on.
  • Human-first design: We augment teams—providing explainable decisions, approvals, and handoff controls.
  • Rapid experimentation: Launch a pilot agent in days, iterate quickly, and scale once you see value.
  • Security at the core: SOC2-ready infrastructure, encrypted data flows, and permission-aware agents.
